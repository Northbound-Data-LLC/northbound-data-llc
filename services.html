Services

Structured engagements and targeted sprints designed to take your analytics infrastructure from where it is to where it needs to be. Each engagement is tailored to your specific context and goals.

Our Engagement Philosophy

Every organization's data challenges are shaped by their specific tools, team structure, and business context. Our engagements start with understanding where you are before we propose where to go.

What stays consistent: clear scope, predictable timelines, and knowledge transfer that ensures your team can maintain what we build.

Analytics Health Assessment

Typical Duration: 2–3 weeks

A comprehensive audit of your analytics ecosystem—what's working, what's broken, and what to prioritize. This is how most of our client relationships begin.

What You Get

  • dbt project audit with health score
  • Metric discrepancy assessment across BI tools
  • Test coverage gap analysis
  • Executive summary with prioritized remediation roadmap

Analytics Foundation Engagement

Typical Duration: 10–20 weeks

A full rebuild of your analytics foundation—the sprawling dbt project, the ungoverned BI queries, the metric definitions that vary by team—transformed into a reliable, maintainable system your team can extend.

What You Get

  • Restructured dbt project with unified metric definitions
  • Migrated and governed BI queries
  • Comprehensive testing, documentation, and CI/CD
  • Team training with 30-day post-engagement support

dbt Greenfield Build

Typical Duration: 8–16 weeks

A complete dbt project built from the ground up—architected for your specific needs with testing, CI/CD, and documentation in place from day one.

What You Get

  • Complete project architecture: sources, staging, business logic, and marts
  • Testing and documentation framework
  • CI/CD pipeline setup
  • Team onboarding and training
