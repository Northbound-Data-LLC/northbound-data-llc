Core Engagements

Structured engagements designed to take your analytics infrastructure from where it is to where it needs to be. Each engagement is tailored to your specific context and goals.

Our Engagement Philosophy

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Every organization's data challenges are shaped by their specific tools, team structure, and business context. Our engagements start with understanding—deeply—where you are before we propose where to go.

What stays consistent is our approach: clear scope, predictable timelines, and knowledge transfer that ensures your team can maintain and extend what we build. We succeed when you don't need us anymore.

Analytics Health Assessment

Typical Duration: 2–3 weeks

Not sure where to start? Start here. A comprehensive audit of your analytics ecosystem that gives you a clear picture of what's working, what's broken, and exactly what to prioritize. This is how most of our client relationships begin—because good decisions require good information.

What You Get

  • dbt project audit with health score
  • BI query inventory and analysis
  • Metric discrepancy assessment
  • Test coverage gap analysis
  • Executive summary presentation
  • Technical findings report
  • Prioritized remediation roadmap
  • Effort estimates for each phase

Best For

  • Teams who know something is wrong but aren't sure what
  • New data leaders inheriting existing infrastructure
  • VPs of Finance questioning data accuracy across reports
  • Organizations preparing for a data transformation
  • Teams seeking executive buy-in for analytics investment

Analytics Foundation Engagement

Typical Duration: 10–20 weeks

A full rebuild of your analytics foundation. We take your existing infrastructure—the sprawling dbt project, the ungoverned BI queries, the metric definitions that vary by team—and transform it into a reliable, maintainable system. Your team will finish the engagement with an analytics infrastructure they understand, trust, and can extend.

What You Get

  • Restructured dbt project following best practices
  • Migrated and governed BI queries
  • Unified metric definitions
  • Comprehensive test coverage
  • Data documentation and dictionary
  • Semantic layer (if applicable)
  • Team training and enablement
  • 30-day post-engagement support

Best For

  • Organizations with existing dbt projects that have grown unwieldy
  • Teams experiencing metric discrepancy issues
  • Finance teams tired of reconciling conflicting numbers across tools
  • Operations leaders who need reliable pipeline and performance data
  • Companies scaling their analytics capabilities

dbt Greenfield Build

Typical Duration: 8–16 weeks

Starting fresh or migrating from a legacy system? We'll design and build a complete dbt project from the ground up—architected for your specific needs with all the foundations in place for long-term success. No technical debt from day one.

What You Get

  • Complete dbt project architecture
  • Source and staging layer
  • Core business logic models
  • Mart models for key domains
  • Testing and documentation
  • CI/CD pipeline setup
  • Team onboarding and training
  • Architecture documentation

Best For

  • Organizations adopting dbt for the first time
  • Teams migrating from legacy transformation tools
  • Companies spinning up new data warehouses
  • CFOs and COOs who need board-ready metrics from day one
  • Startups building analytics from scratch
