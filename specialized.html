Specialized Services

Targeted sprints that solve specific challenges. These services can be standalone engagements or combined with our core offerings for comprehensive transformation.

Metric Alignment Sprint

End the "which number is right?" debates. We audit your key metrics across all systems, identify discrepancies, and implement unified definitions that everyone can trust.

Typical scope: 2–4 weeks
Outcome: Single source of truth for your core metrics
Business impact: Finance, marketing, and operations finally reporting the same numbers

Query Ecosystem Migration

Hundreds of saved queries scattered across Looker, Mode, Tableau, and other tools? We inventory, assess, and migrate your valuable queries into governed, documented, version-controlled logic.

Typical scope: 3–6 weeks
Outcome: Clean, governed query library with clear ownership

Semantic Layer Development

Build a semantic layer that gives your organization consistent metrics and self-service analytics. We implement and configure your semantic layer with proper metric definitions and access patterns.

Typical scope: 4–8 weeks
Outcome: Fully configured semantic layer with governed metrics
Business impact: Non-technical stakeholders get self-service access to trustworthy data

Data Quality & Testing Overhaul

Implement comprehensive testing that catches data issues before your stakeholders do. We design and implement test suites covering freshness, uniqueness, relationships, and business rules.

Typical scope: 2–4 weeks
Outcome: Robust test coverage with clear alerting and remediation paths
Business impact: Executives and board members see correct numbers the first time, every time

Data Profiling & Documentation

Transform tribal knowledge into institutional knowledge. We profile your data, document schemas and transformations, and create a data dictionary your whole organization can use.

Typical scope: 2–4 weeks
Outcome: Complete data documentation and discovery layer

CI/CD Pipeline Implementation

Automate your dbt workflows with proper continuous integration and deployment. We set up testing, documentation generation, and safe deployment practices for your data transformations.

Typical scope: 1–2 weeks
Outcome: Automated testing and deployment pipeline

Mix and match to fit your needs

These services work standalone for focused improvements, or they can be bundled together or added to our core engagements. During our initial conversation, we'll help you identify the combination that addresses your specific challenges.
