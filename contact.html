Common Questions

Do you work with teams of our size?

We work with data teams ranging from solo practitioners to teams of 30+. The specific engagement we recommend depends on your situation, but we've helped organizations of all sizes.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. All of our engagements are remote-first. We've successfully delivered projects for clients across the US and internationally without ever meeting in person.

What's the typical engagement length?

Assessments run 2-3 weeks. Foundation engagements typically run 10-20 weeks depending on scope. Specialized sprints range from 2-8 weeks. We scope based on your specific needs.

Can we start with something small?

Absolutely. Most clients start with an Analytics Health Assessment. It's a contained engagement that gives you clear value (diagnosis and roadmap) while letting us both evaluate whether a longer engagement makes sense.

What if we're not ready to commit yet?

That's fine. Our initial conversation is just that—a conversation. We'll discuss your situation, share some thoughts, and you can decide if and when you want to move forward. No pressure, no follow-up spam.
