Ongoing Support

After an engagement ends, your analytics infrastructure needs ongoing attention. Our retainers provide the expertise you need—whether that's light maintenance or embedded team augmentation.

Why ongoing support matters

Analytics infrastructure isn't a one-time project. Business requirements evolve, new data sources appear, team members change, and the work we do together needs to grow with your organization. A retainer relationship ensures you have expert support when you need it—without the overhead of a full-time hire or the delay of starting a new engagement.

Most of our retainer clients come from completed engagements. They've seen how we work, and they want to keep that level of expertise available as they continue building.
