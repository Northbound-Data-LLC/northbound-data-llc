About Northbound Data

Analytics consulting for data teams who care about getting it right.

Who We Are

Northbound Data is an analytics consulting firm founded by Mona. We specialize in helping data teams build analytics infrastructure that actually works—reliable dbt projects, consistent metrics, and data that organizations can trust.

Before starting Northbound Data, Mona spent over a decade building and leading data teams at high-growth companies. The same problems kept appearing: dbt projects that grew into unmaintainable tangles, metrics that never matched across tools, and data teams that spent more time firefighting than building.

These problems aren't unsolvable. They're just hard to solve from the inside, when you're simultaneously trying to keep the lights on and satisfy stakeholder requests. That's where we come in—external expertise that can diagnose problems clearly and implement solutions systematically.

Our Philosophy

What We Believe

Simplicity over cleverness

The best analytics infrastructure is boring. Clear naming conventions, straightforward logic, obvious structure. Clever solutions create maintenance nightmares. We optimize for understandability, not elegance.

Test what matters

100% test coverage is meaningless if you're testing the wrong things. We focus on tests that catch real issues—business logic violations, data quality problems, silent failures. Quality over quantity.

Documentation as code

Documentation that lives separately from code gets stale. We embed documentation in the same place as the logic it describes, making it easy to maintain and impossible to ignore.

Transfer, don't hoard

Our goal is to make ourselves unnecessary. Every engagement includes knowledge transfer. When we leave, your team should understand everything we built and be confident extending it.

What It's Like to Work With Us

We treat your problems like our problems. When we're engaged, we're thinking about your data infrastructure in the shower, on walks, at 2am when we can't sleep. That's not workaholism—it's just how deep work happens.

We're direct. If something isn't working, we'll tell you. If we think you're heading in the wrong direction, we'll say so. You're not paying us to agree with you—you're paying us for honest expertise.

We're pragmatic. Best practices are guidelines, not laws. What works for a FAANG company with 200 data engineers might not work for your team of 5. We adapt approaches to your context, not the other way around.

We communicate clearly. You'll always know what we're working on, what we've found, and what we recommend. No jargon, no hand-waving, no mysterious processes happening behind the curtain.
